WeAreTheDJ

Let everyone choose the music

Launch a party in one click

One deezer account is needed to play the music

Wearethedj is a collaborative playlist. It allows everyone at the party to vote for the music on their phone.

Demo

Dead simple setup

  1. Create the party in one click
  2. Plug the computer to a sound system
  3. Send the link of the voting page to everyone


That's it! People can now add tracks to the playlist and vote for them. Everytime a track finishes, the top voted one is removed from the playlist and played next.
