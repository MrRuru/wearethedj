WearetheDJ is a collaborative playlist. It allows everyone at the party to vote for the music on their phone.

How it works  ( It's quite easy )

You create a shared playlist with a unique password, plug your computer to your soundsytem, and sign-in with a deezer account

They enter the password you chose, add tracks to the playlist, and vote for them in real time

The music starts when you click "play". When a track finishes, the top one in the playlist plays next automatically.
